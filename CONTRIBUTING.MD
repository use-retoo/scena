# Contributing to @retoo/scena

## How contributions work

By submitting a pull request or any contribution to this repository, you confirm that:

- The code is your own original work, or you have the right to contribute it.
- You grant **Retoo** (https://retoo.ru) a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, modify, and distribute your contribution as part of this project.
- You retain copyright to your contribution, but you may not revoke the above license once granted.

This is a lightweight **DCO-style** approach — no formal agreement to sign, but the act of contributing implies consent to these terms.

## Small contributions

Bug fixes and trivial patches are welcome without any additional process — just open a pull request.

## Questions

Contact us at support@retoo.ru or open an issue in this repository.
